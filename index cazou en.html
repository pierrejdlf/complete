The project aims to present different point of views of the Europe of today. The call will end on 2014 may 20th, before the european elections (22th to 25th) Please consult conditions to participate

  • choose a (from 1 to 5 ?) word

    europe. what does it make you think, inspire, ... you need to build your film around the association between Europe and the word you have choosen

  • form

    it can be a feature-fiction-horror-film, a video portrait, a documentary essay, a small fiction, a photographic collage, ... whatever you want

  • duration (excluding credits)

    the minimum duration is 1 minute
    the maximum duration is 10 minutes

  • subtitles

    you also need to give us an english subtitles file translating the discourse

  • copyrights

    you need to be the copyright owner of all the images and sounds

  • sending

    you can either send us
    - directly the file (wetransfer, dropbox, ...)
    - the link of the vimeo hosted film

  • diffusion & votes

    all the the received films will be available online (from april 20th), in order to allow the public votes

  • awards

    only the films produced in 2014 will be considered.
    • jury award: 1000 euros
    • public award: 400 euros

Un peu plus tôt est un collectif ouvert regroupant une dizaine de jeunes auteurs, cinéastes, designers, ingénieurs européens.

En 2014, avec le projet Europe Moving Image, le collectif souhaite mettre en lien des auteurs (amateurs ou professionnels) qui utilisent l'image et le montage comme langage. L'appel à film Europe in a word est la première étape d’une réflexion plus longue sur la question de l’Europe et de ses images.

Le projet est soutenu par le programme PEJA de l'Union Européenne. Nous remercions particulièrement B. Durey, l'association Nisi Masa pour leur soutien et précieux conseils.
